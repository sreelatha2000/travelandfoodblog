Kabir

Kabir is an Indian academician, author, and psychologist. He has worked as the Deputy Vice Chancellor (Academics and Research) at the Mount Zion International University of Rwanda (MZIUR) ,Kigali, Rwanda, East Africa for the period 26th September 2018 to 31st July 2020. Prior to this he was the Registrar at Kazi Nazrul University, [1] Asansol, West Bengal, India (March 2017 - March 2018). He has been the former principal of Scottish Church College, Kolkata, India (July 2015 - August 2016).[2] He has served as the head of the postgraduate department of psychology at St. John's College, Agra[3] (March 1991 to July 2015). He was teaching graduate and postgraduate classes along with supervision of research work. He had been actively involved in various offbeat research works related with the human psyche. He did his schooling from St. Joseph's College, Nainital and his post-graduation in psychology from the Delhi University.
